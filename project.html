My personal project is a gift I hope to give anyone who struggles spending too much time, money and energy on their meals. Inspired by my experience as a mother who worked hard to feed my family healthy meals every day. I saved money with these homemade meals but spent so much time making menu plans and grocery lists. And then on the other hand watching my husband spend a quarter of our grocery budget on eating out when I'm gone for the weekend. I was lucky to have the time to think through meals each week and plan for them but as my life got busier I didn't want to do it anymore. I started buying premade food and eating out more but that didn't make me feel good. I came up with this system so we can have easy meals that are healthy and inexpensive.
