Intuitive Meals

My personal project is a gift I hope to give anyone who struggles spending too much time, money and energy on their meals.

This project was inspired by my experience as a mother who worked hard to feed my family healthy meals every day. I saved money with these homemade meals but spent too much time making menu plans and grocery lists.

On the other extreme I'd find out that my husband spent a good chunk of our grocery budget on eating out when I was gone for the weekend. We needed to find a balance.

I was lucky to have the time to think through meals each week and plan for them but as my life got busier I couldn't do it anymore. I started buying premade food and eating out more but it was much less healthy and didn't make me feel good.

So I came up with this system where we can have easy meals that are healthy, inexpensive, and unplanned simply by making meals intuitive.


Intuitive Meal Planning

The idea is that you learn to think of meals as a combination of grains, protein, vegetables and sauces. Then...

  1. Identify your favorite foods in each of these catagories.

  2. Fill your pantry, fridge and freezer with all your favorites.

  3. Consult the app for basic dinner ideas in each of the following catagories:

    1. Tacos/Burritos
    2. Stirfry
    3. Soup
    4. Salad
    5. Wraps
    6. Bowls
    7. Sheet Pan
    8. Pizza
Other ideas: add your ingredients to a grocery list that connects to Smith's website and puts them into your shopping cart.
